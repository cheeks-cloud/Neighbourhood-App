About Hood-Ville

We believe in the possibilities nearby and Beyond

By bringing neighbors and organizations together, we can cultivate a kinder world where everyone has a neighborhood they can rely on.

The neighborhood network

Neighbors around the world turn to Hood-Ville daily to get trusted information, give and get help, get things done, and build real-world insights with those nearby — neighbors, businesses, and public services. We believe connecting with other like-minded individuals is a universal human need. That said,the reality that neighborhoods are among the most important communities in our lives, have been guiding principles for Nextdoor services since the beginning.

Connecting with the neighborhood

We recognize that thriving communities are made up of the neighbors and organizations that build stronger, more vibrant, and resilient neighborhoods.

Neighbors — Connect with the people you already wave to in your neighborhood, or those you have yet to meet.
Businesses — Instantly reach your most valuable customers — your neighbors — and become a trusted part of the neighborhood.
Establishments — Engage with the people who need you most and find support from home owners nearby with offers like Mortgages and Flexible Payment Plans.
Public agencies — Share critical, real-time information directly at the neighborhood level.
Large brands — Provide locally relevant products and that are upto standards with the Urban Developemnt Planning Agency.

Step 1

Log-in to our app and select the location where you want to avail the service. By default, the app will automatically pick up your current location using the GPS of your phone. However, you can always set the location manually.

Step 2

Select the service you wanted and when you wanted. The scheduling service option is also available there. So that you can book the service when you are at work and we will be there when you return.

Step 3

Select the payment option, and tap on confirm to book your service. Our representatives will reach your location, at your desired time. The app will also remind you about the service 30 minutes before the time, so you won’t forget.

Live Tracking

You can track the service status anytime from anywhere.

Schedule Service

Changed your mind? Easily schedule your the service you have booked for another time.
